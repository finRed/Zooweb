Meet Rex the Croc

Rex isn't your average crocodile—he's a 1300-pound apex predator with a taste for jazz music and sunbathing on river rocks. At nearly 17 feet long, he rules the winding waterways of the Mangrove Maze, a tangled swamp where only the bold (or the foolish) dare venture. Rex is old—like really old—well over 80 years, with a jawline that could crush bone and eyes that have seen empires fall and rise again. Locals say he's part legend, part ghost, and 100% terrifying if you hear a low rumble just beneath your boat.

Fun Facts about Rex the Croc:

  • Rex weighs an impressive 1300 pounds and measures nearly 17 feet in length, making him one of the largest crocodiles in the region.
  • At over 80 years old, Rex has survived decades in the dangerous waters of the Mangrove Maze.

Rex truly is a legendary creature of the swamp!
