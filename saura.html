Meet Saura the Iguana

Saura is a bright green iguana with sun-kissed scales, a laid-back vibe, and a tail longer than most lizards' life stories. She's a vegetarian queen, chillin' in the treetops of the Mangrove Maze, munching on mango leaves and soaking up rays like it's a full-time job. She's got a spiky back ridge that makes her look a little punk, and eyes that say, "I've seen things... but I'm not stressed about it." While Rex is power and Steve is cunning, Saura is all zen—calm, observant, and surprisingly wise.

Fun Facts about Iguanas (And Saura):

  • Green iguanas like Saura have a "third eye" on top of their heads—it's called a parietal eye. It doesn't see images, but it detects light and movement, helping them spot predators like birds from above.
  • Iguanas are excellent swimmers and can hold their breath underwater for up to 30 minutes when escaping predators.

Saura embodies the peaceful wisdom of her species!
