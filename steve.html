Meet Steve the Snake

Steve is a king cobra with a silver tongue and a golden crown of scales. At over 18 feet long, he's the smoothest talker in the swamp, coiling through the vines with style and precision. Where Rex is brute force, Steve is all finesse—calculated, clever, and just venomous enough to win any argument (or end it permanently).

Fun Facts about King Cobras:

  • King cobras are the longest venomous snakes in the world, capable of reaching lengths of up to 18 feet.
  • Despite their name, king cobras aren't true cobras—they belong to their own genus and are known for eating other snakes.
  • Cobras can "hear" with their jaws—even though they don't have external ears like humans, they sense vibrations through the ground using their jawbones.
  • This unique ability helps them detect approaching threats or prey, even in total darkness.

Steve truly embodies the elegance and mystery of his species!
